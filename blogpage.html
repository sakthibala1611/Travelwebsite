Tourism Blog

Ramanathapuram is a historic district in Tamil Nadu famous for temples, beaches, and cultural heritage.

Dhanushkodi is an abandoned town at the south-eastern tip of Pamban Island in Tamil Nadu, about 24 km west of Talaimannar, Sri Lanka.

Pamban Bridge is a railway bridge connecting Rameswaram on Pamban Island to Mandapam on mainland India.

The Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Memorial is located in Peikarumbu, Rameswaram, built by DRDO as a tribute to the former President of India.

Arulmigu Valividu Murugan Temple is a famous Murugan temple located in Ramanathapuram, Tamil Nadu.

The Kothandaramaswamy Temple at Rameswaram is dedicated to Lord Rama and is one among the 108 Abhimana Kshethram of Vaishnavite tradition.
